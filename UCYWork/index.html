About

NoCode-UCY is the home to applications that are developed using No-Code tools; with the goal of exploring the feasibility of Easy-to-Use tools for "Civilian Developers"

The applications are developed by students and researchers of Data Management Systems Laboratory (DMSL), Department of Computer Science at the University of Cyprus.

To contact us here or at lowcode@ucy.ac.cy.

Our releases are free to use and available on both web browsers and the Google Play Store for your convenience. Source code can be found on our lowcode GitHub page.
